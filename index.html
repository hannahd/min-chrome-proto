Want less browser?

This is a prototype to push the desktop Chrome experience closer to the mobile feel. Try it out! Put Chrome into presentation mode (Mac: View menu) for the best effect. Scroll and click. Make a new tab and browse to the New York Times.

As a prototype, this is not intended to cover all cases, but instead give you a sense of the experience. Sadly, Google and a lot of other popular websites won't work here.
